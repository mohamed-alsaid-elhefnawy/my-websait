CNC 3018 machine project

Project Overview

The CNC 3018 is a highly versatile and precise machine designed for intricate engraving and milling tasks. This project involved the assembly, configuration, and optimization of the CNC 3018 platform to achieve professional-grade results.

By integrating a high-power laser module, the machine was expanded to handle both mechanical milling and precision laser cutting, bridging the gap between subtractive manufacturing and light-based fabrication.

Machine Specifications

Effective Work Area 300 x 180 x 45 mm
Laser Power 2.5W – 5.5W
Power Supply 12V
Accuracy ±0.1 mm
Control Software
LightBurn

Main Components

Aluminum Frame

A high-strength structural skeleton made from precision-machined aluminum extrusions for maximum rigidity and vibration damping.

Laser Module

High-power laser module with adjustable focus, capable of high-detail engraving and precision cutting.

Stepper Motors

NEMA 17 high-torque stepper motors ensuring smooth movement and accurate positioning across all axes.

ESP MSK DLC32 Control Board

Advanced 32-bit control board featuring integrated Wi-Fi for remote operation and high-speed processing.

Working Principle

1

Digital Design

Designs are created in LightBurn and converted into G-code—a language the machine understands.

LightBurn
2

Signal Processing

The 32-bit controller board processes the G-code and sends precise electrical pulses to the stepper motor drivers.

3

Precision Execution

The motors move the laser head along the X and Y axes while the laser intensity is modulated to engrave or cut the material.
