Smart Desert City

Urban Innovation for New Mansoura

Vision & Concept

The Smart Desert City is a visionary urban planning project aimed at transforming arid landscapes into sustainable, technology-driven communities. Proposed for the expansion of New Mansoura, this concept focuses on harmony between nature and high-tech infrastructure.

By utilizing solar energy, advanced water recycling systems, and IoT-enabled public services, the city aims to provide a high quality of life while maintaining a minimal environmental footprint.

The Engineering Framework

The Smart Desert City is built upon a multi-disciplinary framework, integrating various engineering specializations to create a cohesive and sustainable urban environment.

Infrastructure

Civil Engineering

Designing resilient structural systems for harsh desert environments, addressing extreme heat and sand movement through climate-adapted construction.

Smart Grid

Electrical Engineering

Designing and optimizing sustainable solar and wind power generation systems, integrated microgrids, and energy storage solutions to ensure reliable power distribution throughout the desert environment, with advanced smart grid controls for efficiency and stability.

HVAC & Energy

Mechanical Engineering

Developing efficient cooling and ventilation systems to maintain indoor comfort and enhance equipment performance through innovative thermal management.

Automation

Mechatronics Engineering

Integrating smart sensors and centralized control for real-time monitoring, enhancing system performance and operational safety.

Air Systems

Aerospace Engineering

Designing drone airports and automated air traffic management systems to enable safe and efficient aerial operations.

Power Generation

Petroleum & Gas Engineering

Designing efficient energy conversion systems using natural gas to ensure resource sustainability and reduce emissions.
